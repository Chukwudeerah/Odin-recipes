Chicken stew

DESCRIPTION

A stew well prepared by Nigerians using chicken as the enticing aspect of the food.

This stew is best known to go down with different dishes.

Example: Rice, Spaghetti, Beans, Noodles e.t.c.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Peppers
  • Salt
  • Maggi
  • Seasonings
  • Full chicken
  • Onions and ginger
  • Groundnut Oil
  • Curry and thyme
  • Tomatoe paste
Steps in preparing a Chicken stew
  1. First cook the chicken using the curry and thyme
  2. Add salt and maggi to the chicken and a little water, allow to steam for 5mins.
  3. When the chicken is done. Place an empty pot on the fire and allow to dry.
  4. Pour a little cup of groundnut oil into the empty pot and allow to heat up.
  5. Then put the sliced onion and pepper into the pot and stir
  6. Put the tomato paste and stir for some minutes, then pour the blended fresh tomatoes.
  7. Stir for 15minutes to make sure the tomatoes are well fried then pour the water from the chicken and stir
  8. pour the chicken and add salt, seasonings, ginger and maggi to taste
  9. Add more water and allow to steam and thick.
  10. If you have a vegetable or curry leave to put, now is the best time. Stir and take down.
  11. Food is Ready!
